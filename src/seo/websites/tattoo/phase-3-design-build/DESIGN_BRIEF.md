# Design Brief — Studio AZ Tattoo Website

**Date:** March 23, 2026
**Domain:** tattooshopminneapolis.com
**Stack:** Next.js 15 + TypeScript + Tailwind CSS 4 + ShadCN → Vercel

---

## Creative Direction

**Core concept:** "Tech meets tattoo" — A collision between clean, geometric tech-company web design and traditional tattoo culture elements. Modern and streamlined approach to tattoos.

### Color Palette
- **Base:** Dark (near-black backgrounds)
- **Primary accent:** Deep/dark purple (plum-range, not vibrant/electric)
- **Secondary accent:** Explore both options:
  - Option A: Warm off-white/cream (sophisticated, high contrast)
  - Option B: Cool electric teal/cyan (futuristic tech edge)
- **Text:** High-contrast light text on dark backgrounds

### Typography
- **Brand font:** Helvetica Light (used in the logo — do not clash with this)
- **Body/UI:** Clean geometric sans-serif (Inter, Satoshi, Geist, or similar)
- **No blackletter** — would clash with Helvetica Light logo
- The tattoo culture element should come from visual design, not typography

### Tattoo Culture Elements (non-typography)
Instead of tattoo-style fonts, inject tattoo DNA through:
- **Geometric line art** — Thin, precise linework in the style of tattoo flash sheets but rendered geometrically (botanical outlines, sacred geometry). Used as section dividers, background textures, hover states.
- **Stipple/dot shading** — Dot-gradient or halftone pattern effects on backgrounds or image overlays. Unmistakably tattoo without touching typography.
- **Needle-thin rule lines** — Ultra-thin 1px decorative lines as section separators, borders, framing elements. Evokes tattoo needle precision.
- **Ink-on-skin textures** — Subtle grain or paper-like texture overlays on certain sections for tactile feel.
- **Flash sheet layout moments** — Gallery/portfolio section borrowing the grid composition of traditional flash sheets, rendered in the modern dark/purple palette.

### Layout & Spatial Design
- **Tech-company grid:** Crisp, generous whitespace, clean spatial rhythm
- **Organic breaks:** Portfolio images bleed edge-to-edge or slightly overlap the grid
- **Mobile-first:** 48px touch targets, 16px body text minimum

### Brand Personality
- "You want a tattoo shop that doesn't feel like you just walked into the back room of a biker bar?"
- Calm, modern, clean, communal
- Approachable but premium
- Confidence without arrogance
- Copy can have personality/edge — the current brand voice has humor

### Competitive Positioning
- No competitor uses dark theme + purple
- No competitor has video on their website
- No competitor has tech-company-level design
- Most competitors are generic Squarespace/Wix templates or grungy tattoo aesthetics
- This design should be immediately distinguishable from everything in the Minneapolis market
