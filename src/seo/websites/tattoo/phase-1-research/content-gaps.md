**Our Opportunity:** We have a 12-step consultation widget that's more sophisticated than any competitor's booking flow. Content about the process:
- What happens during a consultation
- How custom designs are created
- Deposit and pricing transparency

- Timeline from consultation to completion

This content targets:

- "How to book a tattoo appointment"

## Gap #5: FAQ Depth

**Who has one:**
| Competitor | FAQ | Questions |
|-----------|-----|-----------|
| Nokomis | On homepage | 6 questions |
| Jackalope | Dedicated page | Unknown count |
| Minneapolis Tattoo Shop | Dedicated page | Unknown count |
| Uptown | Dedicated page | Unknown count |
| Timeless | "F&Q" page | Unknown count |
| Others | None | — |

**Our Opportunity:** A comprehensive FAQ page with 15-20 questions covering:

**Booking & Pricing:**
- How much does a tattoo cost?
- Do I need an appointment?
- What's the deposit amount?
- How far in advance should I book?
- Do you accept walk-ins?

**First Timers:**
- Does getting a tattoo hurt?
- How do I prepare for my first tattoo?
- What should I wear to my appointment?
- Can I bring someone with me?
- How old do I have to be?

**The Tattoo Process:**
- How long does a tattoo take?
- Can I bring my own design?
- What if I don't have a design?
- Do you do cover-ups?
- What styles do your artists specialize in?

**Aftercare:**
- How do I care for my new tattoo?
- When can I swim after getting a tattoo?
- How long does healing take?
- Do you offer touch-ups?

**About Studio AZ:**
- Where are you located?
- Where do I park?
- Do you speak Spanish? (¿Hablan español?)

Each question = potential featured snippet + People Also Ask box. Use FAQPage schema for all.

## Gap #6: Neighborhood/Local Content

**Nobody creates neighborhood content.** Every competitor has an address and hours. Nobody writes about their neighborhood, nearby landmarks, transit options, or "why this location."

**Our Opportunity (Parking Page):**
- North Loop neighborhood guide (restaurants, bars, things to do before/after appointment)
- Parking options (specific lots, street parking tips, metered vs free)
- Transit directions (bus lines, light rail)
- Walking directions from landmarks
- This targets "tattoo shop North Loop" and "tattoo near downtown Minneapolis"

**Why this matters:** Google rewards local content signals. Mentioning the North Loop, Warehouse District, Target Field, etc. helps Google understand our geographic relevance.

## Gap #7: Spanish/Bilingual Content

**Zero competitors offer Spanish content.** Minneapolis has a significant Spanish-speaking population, and Studio AZ's consultation widget is already bilingual.

**Our Opportunity:**
- Not full site translation (too much maintenance), but key pages with Spanish sections:
  - Home page: Spanish welcome section
  - Services: Spanish service descriptions
  - FAQ: Spanish FAQ section
  - Contact: Spanish hours/directions
- `hreflang` tags for Google's language targeting
- This targets: "tatuajes Minneapolis", "salón de tatuajes Minneapolis"
