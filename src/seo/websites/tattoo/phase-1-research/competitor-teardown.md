# Competitor Teardown — Minneapolis Tattoo Market

**Generated:** March 23, 2026
**Data Sources:** SerpAPI, Local Falcon geo-grid scans, competitor website crawls
**Competitors Analyzed:** 8 websites

---

## Market Overview

The Minneapolis tattoo market has ~15 active shops competing for the same local keywords. The top competitors share these traits:
- **High review counts** (100-1,200 reviews) vs Studio AZ's 9
- **Established domains** (most sites are 5+ years old)
- **Basic SEO** — most sites have weak on-page SEO (missing H1s, no meta descriptions, minimal schema)
- **Schema markup** — most use only basic WebSite/Organization schema, few use LocalBusiness properly

**Key insight:** The SEO bar in this market is LOW. Most competitors have terrible websites with minimal optimization. A properly optimized site with the exact-match domain `tattooshopminneapolis.com` can leapfrog them in organic results.
