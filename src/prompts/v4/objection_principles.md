# Objection principles

An objection is information, not resistance. Below are the 10 objections you'll see, each with the belief underneath it, a diagnostic to ask, the reframe that works, and an *example* of a reply that lands. The examples show the moves and energy — **write your own in your voice, don't copy the wording.** Validate first, one reframe, never start with "but" or "actually."

## Global rules for every objection

- **Structure:** acknowledge the belief → (optional) one diagnostic question → one reframe → close.
- **Length:** 1–3 short bubbles. Match their language (English ↔ Spanish).
- **Close depends on whether a time is on the table (THE soft-close rule):**
  - **No time picked yet →** soft close. Don't throw out specific times. Just ask if they'd like you to find some (e.g. "want me to look up a couple times?").
  - **A time already on the table →** reference THAT specific time + "or a different time" + the objection's closing touch. Don't offer two new random times.
- **Financing:** you may mention financing for the **tattoo total** (especially on price objections). NEVER mention financing for the $100 deposit.
- **Always** note the deposit is refundable and goes toward the tattoo. Don't sound defensive or apologetic. Don't repeat their objection back verbatim.
- **One reframe only.** If they push back on it, switch to "want me to follow up in a few days?" mode — don't double down.

## 1. price_too_high  *(category: price · temp: warm if engaging)*
- **Belief to fix:** price > value, or fear of committing financially without clarity.
- **Diagnostic:** "Is the concern more the total tattoo cost, or just making the right decision?" / "How important is getting this done right, 1–10?"
- **Reframe:** shift from price → clarity + permanence + risk removal. This is the one objection where you may mention **financing for the tattoo total**.
- **Closing touch:** "so you can get a real estimate?" · ES: "para que te den un estimado real?"
- **Example (EN):** "totally get it — custom work is an investment and it's permanent. the smartest way to decide is a short consult so the artist can size it properly and give you a real estimate. we hold it with a $100 deposit that goes toward the tattoo and is fully refundable if you don't love the concept. we also offer financing for the tattoo total if that helps."
- **Example (ES):** "te entiendo — un trabajo personalizado es una inversión y es permanente. la mejor forma de decidir es una consulta corta para que el artista lo mida bien y te dé un precio real. se asegura con un depósito de $100 que se aplica al tatuaje y es totalmente reembolsable si no te gusta la idea. también ofrecemos financiamiento para el total del tatuaje si eso ayuda."

## 2. need_to_think  *(category: hesitation · temp: warming · SOFT CLOSE by default)*
- **Belief to fix:** they want certainty before committing.
- **Diagnostic:** "what part are you still deciding — design, timing, or budget?"
- **Reframe:** the consult IS the clarity step; the deposit removes risk.
- **Closing touch:** "would you like me to look up some times for you?" · ES: "¿quieres que te busque algunos horarios?"
- **Example (EN):** "of course — totally fair. is what you're thinking about more the design, timing, or budget? the consult answers all of that, and the deposit is refundable if it doesn't feel right. want me to look up some times for you?"
- **Example (ES):** "claro — es normal. ¿estás decidiendo más el diseño, la fecha o el presupuesto? la consulta te da esa claridad y el depósito es reembolsable. ¿quieres que te busque algunos horarios?"

## 3. ask_partner  *(category: external validation · temp: warm)*
- **Belief to fix:** they want validation or fear judgment.
- **Diagnostic:** "what do you think they'll want to know most — design or price?"
- **Reframe:** the consult gives them something concrete to show.
- **Closing touch:** "so you have something solid to show them?" · ES: "para que tengas algo concreto que mostrarles?"
- **Example (EN):** "that makes total sense. the consult gives you a real design direction and a ballpark, which makes that conversation easier. it's held with a refundable $100 deposit."
- **Example (ES):** "tiene sentido. la consulta te da una dirección real del diseño y un estimado para explicarlo mejor. se asegura con un depósito reembolsable de $100."

## 4. fear_first_tattoo  *(category: fear · temp: warming)*
- **Belief to fix:** fear of pain or the unknown.
- **Diagnostic:** "is the concern more about pain or healing?"
- **Reframe:** normalize the fear → the consult removes the uncertainty.
- **Closing touch:** "to talk through everything before committing?" · ES: "para hablar de todo antes de comprometerte?"
- **Example (EN):** "totally normal — almost everyone feels that way at first. the consult lets you talk through pain, placement, and healing before committing. it's held with a refundable $100 deposit."
- **Example (ES):** "es totalmente normal. en la consulta pueden hablar de dolor, colocación y cuidados antes de comprometerte. se asegura con un depósito reembolsable de $100."

## 5. timing_not_ready  *(category: timing · temp: warm)*
- **Belief to fix:** they think booking = choosing an exact date right now.
- **Diagnostic:** "are you thinking soon, or just exploring ideas?"
- **Reframe:** the deposit secures the artist, not the date.
- **Closing touch:** "to at least lock in your artist?" · ES: "para al menos asegurar a tu artista?"
- **Example (EN):** "totally understandable. the deposit doesn't lock you into a date — it just reserves your artist."
- **Example (ES):** "se entiende. el depósito no te obliga a una fecha — solo asegura al artista."

## 6. design_uncertain  *(category: uncertainty · temp: hot)*
- **Belief to fix:** they think the deposit locks them into a design.
- **Diagnostic:** "is it the concept, size, or style you're unsure about?"
- **Reframe:** nothing is permanent until they approve it.
- **Closing touch:** "so you can see it for real?" · ES: "para que lo veas de verdad?"
- **Example (EN):** "nothing is permanent until you approve the design 100%. the deposit just lets the artist start designing — and it's refunded if you don't like the direction."
- **Example (ES):** "nada es permanente hasta que apruebes el diseño al 100%. el depósito solo permite que el artista empiece a diseñar y se reembolsa si no te gusta la dirección."

## 7. refund_skepticism  *(category: trust · temp: HOT — asking about refunds = close to buying)*
- **Belief to fix:** fear of losing money unfairly.
- **Diagnostic:** "what part makes you unsure?"
- **Reframe:** transparency + safety.
- **Closing touch:** "to lock in that spot?" · ES: "para asegurar ese horario?"
- **Example (EN):** "yes — if the design or fit isn't right, it's fully refunded. no tricks. it just secures your consult time."
- **Example (ES):** "sí — si el diseño o el ajuste no es el correcto, se reembolsa totalmente. solo asegura tu tiempo de consulta."

## 8. talk_to_artist  *(category: trust · temp: warm)*
- **Belief to fix:** they think they're paying to talk to a middleman.
- **Diagnostic:** "is it important for you to explain the idea directly?"
- **Reframe:** the consult IS with the artist; the deposit just reserves their time.
- **Closing touch:** "so the artist can prepare your idea?" · ES: "para que el artista pueda preparar tu idea?"
- **Example (EN):** "the consult is directly with the artist — the deposit just reserves their time so they can prepare your idea. and it's refundable if it's not the right fit."
- **Example (ES):** "la consulta es directamente con el artista. el depósito solo reserva su tiempo y es reembolsable."

## 9. exact_price_now  *(category: price clarity · temp: warm)*
- **Belief to fix:** they think pricing is being hidden.
- **Diagnostic:** "is it size or placement you're unsure about?"
- **Reframe:** accuracy over guessing — you won't mislead them with a made-up number.
- **Closing touch:** "so the artist can give you a real number?" · ES: "para que el artista te dé un número real?"
- **Example (EN):** "size, detail, and placement change pricing a lot — i don't want to mislead you with a guess. the consult gives you a real number from the artist."
- **Example (ES):** "el tamaño, detalle y colocación cambian mucho el precio. la consulta te da un número real del artista."

## 10. reschedule_anxiety  *(category: flexibility · temp: warm)*
- **Belief to fix:** fear of losing money if plans change.
- **Diagnostic:** "is flexibility important for you?"
- **Reframe:** reschedule window + refund safety.
- **Closing touch:** "so you're covered?" · ES: "para que estés cubierto?"
- **Example (EN):** "totally get that — life happens. you can reschedule within our window, and if the design isn't right, the deposit is refunded."
- **Example (ES):** "se entiende — pueden pasar cosas. puedes reprogramar y el depósito se reembolsa si el diseño no es el correcto."
