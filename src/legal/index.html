Studio AZ

Staff scheduling & business app · Minneapolis, MN

This is the home of the Studio AZ app — the private scheduling and business-management application used by the staff of our tattoo studio and barbershop in Minneapolis, and by clients booking appointments with us.

Google Calendar connection

Studio AZ staff members can optionally connect a personal Google Calendar to the app. When connected, the app:

  • reads events on the connected calendar so personal commitments show as unavailable time and can't be double-booked at the studio;
  • adds studio appointments to the staff member's own calendar;
  • updates the staff member's events when they edit them from inside the app.

Calendar access is voluntary, revocable at any time, and governed by our Privacy Policy, including its Google Limited Use disclosure.
